Neural Martingale Dual Bounds live

Primal-dual bracketing of Bermudan put prices under GBM. Lower bound via Longstaff-Schwartz, upper bound via a neural martingale trained to minimize the Rogers (2002) dual objective. Martingale property enforced by construction using a stochastic integral parametrization — no regularization required.

key results — ATM put, S₀=K=100, σ=0.2, T=1
Binomial reference6.0043
LSMC lower bound5.8782
Neural upper bound6.4702 ± 0.04
Dual gap0.59  (−95% vs Doob)
